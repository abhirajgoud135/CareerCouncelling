Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock. It includes the preparation of plants and animals products for people to use and their distribution to markets. Or The science, art or practice of cultivating soil, producing crops, and raising livestock and varying degrees the preparation and marketing of the resulting products cleared to use the land for agriculture. The main source of raw materials to major industries such as cotton, fabric, sugar, tobacco, edible as well as non - edible oils in agriculture. Moreover, many other industries such as processing of fruits as well as vegetables and rice husking get their raw material mainly from agriculture.
